CodeStratum

Forward

A mountain is made of many individual layers of earth, each built on top of the last.

The layer all others rest on is called the statum, and the tallest mountains have the strongest strata.

Software knowledge is similarly built; High-level concepts are derived from atomic pieces, or layers.

Strengthen your stratum.

Objective

If you find yourself wondering "am I doing this right?" then you are on the right track toward mastery of the topic at hand.

What you realize you are lacking is the most effective learning tool; directly relevant advice from experts.

CodeStratum aims to provide that advice at the moment you need it most, when you are building your foundational base.

As a learner:

  • Push yourself to master fundamentals while also actualizing your goals
  • Access expert advice that you may not otherwise have access to

As an expert:

  • Create deeply engaging learning experiences in a variety of domains
  • Strengthen your education skills in a new way

What's Next?

No work has been done on this project yet, it is still very much in its infancy. Some ideas I have in no order or regard for complexity:

  • GitHub PR reviews seems like a good feature for providing the actual feedback
  • Recognize that many early learners will not use PRs on their projects, have a commit workflow in mind
  • If it's commits that are reviewed, of course it's up to the learner seeking expert advice to have an acceptable git discipline
  • The ratio between learner and expert will always be skewed of course
